Cybersecurity

Information Security Analyst

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $70,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $85,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $105,000/year
  • Job Description: Works on the maintenance, monitoring and support of information security initiatives, and password management. Additionally, you would be monitoring and protecting networks from cyber threats and will be installing security tools.
  • Lifestyle: As an Information Security Analyst you will be able to work remote with some on-call work required during breaches, installation of security tools. Work hours are not as flexible as other jobs, typically will be working Monday-Friday, and work on weekends, holidays, and non-business hours may be required.

Penetration Tester (Ethical Hacker)

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $85,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $105,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $125,000/year
  • Job Description: Will be conducting manual penetration testing against web applications, APIs, cloud environments, infrastructure, mobile applications and custom technologies. You will also be providing security advice and partnership to engineering teams and non-technical partners. Will be developing scripts and tools to augment penetration testing activites. To summarize, you will be simulating cyberattacks to identify security weaknesses in systems and then reporting these weaknesses.
  • Lifestyle: As a Penetration Tester you will usually be working remotely, but some companies may require you to work on-site. Usually will have flexible hours but would still require working 40 hour weeks. Additionally, the jobs are project-based, but may involve tight deadlines for clients and will require communication between other workers.

Security Consultant

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $95,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $120,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $140,000/year
  • Job Description: Will be supporting teams of professionals working to evaluate and secure infrastructures. As a Security Consultant you will be assessing and improving organizations security systems and you will be providing expert advice.
  • Lifestyle: As a Security Consultant you will typically be working with multiple clients and frequent travel may be required. The work hours are flexible and is mostly remote.
