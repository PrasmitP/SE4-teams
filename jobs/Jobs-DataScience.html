Data Science

Data Analyst

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $60,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $75,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $90,000/year
  • Job Description: Optimizes project workflows by taking large quantities of unusable data and converting it into usable dashboards. Essentially, a Data Analyst cleans and analyzes data to help organizations make better decisions.
  • Lifestyle: A Data Analyst is typically office-based but can work remotely, with regular hours. As a Data Analyst, you will attend meetings and work in a team environment.

Machine Learning Engineer

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $100,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $120,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $140,000/year
  • Job Description: Uses expertise in mechanical engineering and physics to develop intelligent systems that mimic real-world scenarios. Designs and trains algorithms that allow computers to learn and make predictions.
  • Lifestyle: A Machine Learning Engineer works on project-based tasks with flexible hours and hybrid working conditions. Continuous learning and model refinement are key aspects of the job.

Data Scientist

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $95,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $115,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $135,000/year
  • Job Description: Uses advanced statistical and analytical techniques such as data mining, algorithms, and machine learning principles. These techniques are typically applied to large and complex data sets, which are then used to support business decisions.
  • Lifestyle: A Data Scientist has flexible hours and often works remotely. As a Data Scientist, you will focus on extracting insights from data to identify opportunities and inform decisions. You will gather and analyze information from multiple sources, spot trends, communicate findings, and provide recommendations to stakeholders.
