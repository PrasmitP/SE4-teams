Software Development

Full Stack Developer

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $80,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $100,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $120,000/year
  • Job Description: Works on the development and maintenance of both the front-end and back-end of applications, often in team environments.
  • Lifestyle: Full Stack Developers typically work 40 to 50 hours a week and often have a hybrid work arrangement, with some days remote and others in-person. Full Stack Developers work in collaborative team settings, handle projects with deadlines, and generally have flexible hours.

Mobile Application Developer

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $75,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $95,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $115,000/year
  • Job Description: Specializes in building and maintaining apps for iOS and Android platforms, often working in team environments.
  • Lifestyle: Mobile Application Developers have flexible hours and often work in a hybrid setup, with some remote and in-person days. The workload can vary depending on project deadlines and the urgency of app updates.

DevOps Engineer

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $90,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $110,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $130,000/year
  • Job Description: Ensures smooth integration between development and operations, automating processes where necessary. DevOps Engineers are responsible for managing the build system, defining and creating test processes, re-platforming, and overseeing the software release system.
  • Lifestyle: DevOps Engineers usually work in a hybrid setup, with some on-call work required. The role is fast-paced, project-based, and team-oriented. Flexibility depends on the employer.
