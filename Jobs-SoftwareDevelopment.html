Software Development

Full Stack Developer

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $80,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $100,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $120,000/year
  • Job Description: Works on the development and maintenance of both front-end and back-end of applications, will also be working in team environments.
  • Lifestyle: Full Stack Developers have a typically work schedule of 40 to 50 hours a week, and can also be done as Hybrid, where you can be remote some days but in-person on other days. Full Stack Developers will be in a collaborative team environment, have projects with deadlines, and has flexible hours.

Mobile Application Developer

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $75,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $95,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $115,000/year
  • Job Description: Specializes in building and maintaining apps for iOS/Android and will be working in team environments.
  • Lifestyle: Mobile Application Developers have flexible hours and can be done as Hybrid, where you can be remote some days but in-person on other days. The workload for a Mobile Application Developer can also vary based on the project deadlines and urgency of updates.

DevOps Engineer

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $90,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $110,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $130,000/year
  • Job Description: Ensures smooth integration between development, operations, and automating processes. DevOps Engineers are responsible for managing the build system, defining/creating and managing the test processes, manages re-platforming and the software release system.
  • Lifestyle: DevOps Engineers jobs are usually done as Hybrid since there are some on-call work that may be required. It is fast-paced, project-based, team-based, and the work flexibility depends on the employer.
