Data Science

Data Analyst

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $60,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $75,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $90,000/year
  • Job Description: Optimizes project flows by taking large quantities of unusable data and converting it into usuable dashboards. Essentially a Data Analyst cleans and analyzes data to help organizations make better decisions.
  • Lifestyle: A Data Analyst is office-based and can be done remote with regular hours. As a Data Analyst you would be doing meetings and working in a team environment.

Machine Learning Engineer

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $100,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $120,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $140,000/year
  • Job Description: Uses expertise in mechanical engineering and physics to develop intelligent systems that mimic the real world. Designs and trains algorithms that allow computers to learn and make predictions.
  • Lifestyle: A Machine Learning Engineer will be doing project-based work with flexible hours that also has Hybrid working conditions. A Machine Learning Engineer will also have to be continuously learning and doing model refinement.

Data Scientist

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $95,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $115,000/year
  • Senior-Level Average Salary: $135,000/year
  • Job Description: Uses advanced statistical and analytical techniques such as data mining, algorithms, and machine learning principles. Typically these techniques will be used on large and complex data sets that will be then used to support business decisions.
  • Lifestyle: A Data Scientist has flexible hours and is often remote work. As a Data Scientist you will be focused on extracting meaning from data and to identify opportunities to inform decisions, you will also be gathering and analyzing information from multiple sources to spot trends and communicating this, and providing recommendations, to stakeholders.
