Data Science

Data Analyst

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $60,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $75,000/year
  • Senior Average Salary: $90,000/year
  • Job Description: Optimizes project flows by taking large quantities of unusable data and converting it into usuable dashboards. Essentially a Data Analyst cleans and analyzes data to help organizations make better decisions.
  • Lifestyle: Office-based with regular hours; workload peaks during reports and projects.

Machine Learning Engineer

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $100,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $120,000/year
  • Senior Average Salary: $140,000/year
  • Job Description: Designs and trains algorithms that allow computers to learn and make predictions.
  • Lifestyle: Project-based work with flexible hours; continuous learning and model refinement.

Data Scientist

  • Entry-Level Average Salary: $95,000/year
  • Mid-Level Average Salary: $115,000/year
  • Senior Average Salary: $135,000/year
  • Job Description: Uses analytics and statistical techniques to interpret complex data for business decisions.
  • Lifestyle: Flexible hours, often remote work; focused on experimentation and optimizing models.
